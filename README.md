# NARRATOR

**A lightweight, privacy-first AI reading companion that works entirely offline.**

## What is NARRATOR?

NARRATOR transforms your documents into an immersive reading and listening experience. It's a calm, focused reading sanctuary that runs completely in your browser — no accounts, no cloud, no tracking.

## Core Features

- **📄 Multi-format Reader** — Upload PDF, TXT, Markdown, or HTML files. Paste text directly.
- **🔊 Intelligent Narration** — Sentence-aware text-to-speech with pause/resume, speed control, and sleep timer.
- **🧠 Local AI Analysis** — Summaries, topic extraction, key sentence detection — all running locally.
- **💬 Contextual Chat** — Ask questions about your document. The assistant searches semantically and admits when it can't find answers.
- **📑 Smart Search** — Debounced search with highlights, match navigation, and result counting.
- **📝 Notes & Highlights** — Built-in notes panel with localStorage persistence.
- **🎧 Audiobook Controls** — Chapter-aware playback, skip forward/back, auto-scroll synchronization.
- **🎬 Cinematic Mode** — Distraction-free dark reading environment with optional ambient sounds (rain, lo-fi, forest).
- **🍅 Pomodoro Timer** — Built-in focus timer for structured reading sessions.
- **📊 Reading Stats** — Word count, paragraph count, estimated read time, and reading streak tracking.
- **📱 Mobile-Friendly** — Responsive design that works on all screen sizes.
- **🔒 100% Private** — Everything stays on your device. No internet required after initial page load.

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser
2. Upload a document or click "Try Demo Text"
3. Read, listen, search, and explore with the AI assistant
4. Your notes and reading history persist across sessions

## Technology

- **Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript** — No frameworks, no build steps
- **PDF.js** — Client-side PDF text extraction
- **Web Speech API** — Native browser text-to-speech
- **IndexedDB + localStorage** — Persistent local storage
- **DOMPurify** — XSS prevention and content sanitization
- **Service Worker** — Offline-ready PWA support

## Philosophy

NARRATOR is designed to feel like:
- A reading sanctuary, not a chatbot interface
- A calm study companion, not a productivity app
- An intelligent audiobook workspace, not a cloud service

The assistant behaves like a focused mentor — helpful, quiet, and honest about its confidence levels. It won't hallucinate or pretend to know things it doesn't.

## Browser Support

Works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). The best narration experience is on Chrome and Edge due to broader voice support.
